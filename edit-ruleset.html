Formula Reference
Formulas are written in plain math. Supported: + - * / ^ (power), parentheses, and functions floor(), ceil(), round(), min(), max(), abs(). Results are floored automatically unless a stat is flagged to keep decimals (like Reflex). See below for available variables.
